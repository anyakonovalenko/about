Teaching assistant for Statistics course with 100+ students at Molde University College.

## Course Topics

* Descriptive statistics and data visualization
* Probability theory and distributions
* Hypothesis testing and statistical inference
* Confidence intervals and parameter estimation
* Regression analysis and correlation
* Statistical software and practical applications

## Responsibilities

* Conducting problem-solving sessions
* Assisting with statistical software training (R, Python, SPSS)
* Grading exams and assignments
* Providing consultation hours for student questions
* Developing practice problems and solutions

## Key Learning Outcomes

Students develop strong statistical foundations necessary for research and data analysis, understanding both theoretical concepts and practical applications through real-world datasets and logistics-related examples.
