This paper presents a comprehensive benchmark study comparing specialized MaxSAT solvers with general-purpose optimization approaches for discrete optimization problems.

## Key Contributions

- Benchmark evaluation of multiple MaxSAT solver implementations
- Demonstration of 22% performance improvement with specialized solvers
- Analysis of solver characteristics and problem type suitability
- Practical recommendations for solver selection

## Findings

Specialized MaxSAT solvers significantly outperform cross-domain approaches, particularly on structured discrete optimization problems common in logistics and operations research applications.
